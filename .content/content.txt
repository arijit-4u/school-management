1. Admission Procedure
Welcome to [School Name] Admissions
Thank you for considering [School Name] for your child’s education. We are committed to providing a nurturing and enriching learning environment for all students. Our admission process is designed to be simple and transparent.

Step-by-Step Admission Process
Step 1: Inquiry & School Visit
Parents/guardians are encouraged to explore our website and visit the school campus.
To schedule a tour, please fill out the Inquiry Form or contact our admissions office at [Phone Number] or [Email Address].
Step 2: Application Submission
Download the application form from our website or collect it from the school office.
Submit the completed application form along with the required documents:
Birth certificate
Recent passport-size photographs
Previous academic records (if applicable)
Address proof of parents/guardians
Any other relevant documents
Step 3: Interaction/Assessment
For Preschool & Kindergarten: An informal interaction with the child and parents.
For Grades 1 & Above: A basic assessment may be conducted to understand the child’s learning level.
Step 4: Admission Confirmation
Selected candidates will receive an admission confirmation via email or call.
Parents must complete the fee payment and submit any pending documents within the given timeframe.
Step 5: Orientation & Enrollment
Once the admission is confirmed, an orientation session will be conducted for new students and parents.
The school ID card, uniform details, and academic calendar will be provided.
