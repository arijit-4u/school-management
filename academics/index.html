ACADEMICS

At Public High School, we take pride in offering diverse and comprehensive academic programs designed to nurture every child's potential and passion for learning. Our range of programs is tailored to provide a well-rounded education that caters to individual needs, encourages critical thinking, and fosters holistic development.

Early Years Program

Our Early Years Program focuses on providing a nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 2 to 5 years. Through a blend of play-based learning, interactive activities, and a carefully curated curriculum, we lay the foundation for academic excellence while nurturing creativity and social skills.

Primary Years Program (Grade 1 - Grade 5)

In the Primary Years, we emphasize a balanced approach to education that encompasses core subjects alongside extracurricular activities. Our curriculum is structured to encourage curiosity, exploration, and independent thinking while ensuring a strong foundation in language, mathematics, science, social studies, and the arts.

Middle Years Program (Grade 6 - Grade 8)

The Middle Years Program is a crucial phase where students transition into more specialized subjects while continuing to develop their critical thinking abilities. With a focus on academic rigor and personal growth, students engage in a variety of subjects and extracurricular opportunities that prepare them for higher studies and real-world challenges.

High School Program (Grade 9 - Grade 12)

Our High School Program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed for higher education and future careers. Through a blend of challenging coursework, comprehensive academic support, and guidance in extracurricular pursuits, we prepare students to excel academically and become well-rounded individuals ready to contribute to society.

Co-curricular and Extra-curricular Activities

Beyond the classroom, Public High School offers a rich array of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. These include sports, arts, music, drama, clubs, and community service initiatives, providing students with opportunities to explore their interests and talents beyond academics.

Individualized Support and Enrichment

We believe in providing personalized attention to each student's needs. Our teachers and support staff are dedicated to identifying and nurturing the strengths of every child while providing additional support where needed to ensure their success.

Learning Key Concepts

Learning at Public High School goes beyond rote memorization. Our emphasis on Learning Key Concepts encourages students to delve deeper into fundamental ideas across various subjects. This approach fosters a strong conceptual understanding, enabling students to apply their knowledge creatively and critically in different contexts.


At Public High School, we strive to create a dynamic learning environment that inspires a lifelong love for learning and prepares students to navigate an ever-changing world with confidence and resilience.
