Admission Procedure:

Welcome to Public High School! Follow these simple steps to complete the admission process.

  • Step 1: Obtain Admission Form

    Visit the school office during working hours or download the form from our "Admissions" section.

  • Step 2: Fill Out the Form

    Complete the form accurately with all the required details.

  • Step 3: Submit Documents

    Submit the form along with:

    • Birth certificate
    • Previous academic records (if applicable)
    • Passport-size photographs
    • Address proof
    • Any other requested documents

  • Step 4: Pay Admission Fee

    Pay the processing fee online or in person and retain the receipt.

  • Step 5: Admission Test/Interview

    We'll inform you about any necessary test or interview dates. Arrive prepared!

  • Step 6: Notification of Results

    Stay tuned for results through email, phone, or our website.

  • Step 7: Final Enrollment

    Complete the enrollment process by paying the fees, signing agreements, and collecting materials.

  • Step 8: Orientation

    Join the orientation session to get familiarized with our school culture and values.
